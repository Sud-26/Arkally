Green Zones & Accident Safety

ArkallyGPS Security
Arkally Green Zones provide useful features for a safe and secure ride to business and personal watercraft owners as well. Safe zones can be created to enjoy Riding freely without any speed limit. If you cross the green zone the warning Beeps will be used to indicate that you out of the green zone.
Moreover, to be out of the green zone Arkally GPS will automatically reduce speed to the minimum you allow. In any kind of critical situation, Arkally GPS allows control of watercraft engines simultaneously to the owner if needed.

Intelligent Distance Control (IDC) System

ArkallyGPS Security
You can achieve more trust and sales by your customers by introducing Intelligent Distance Control (IDC). You can save time, money, and nerves. Intelligent Distance Control (IDC) system reduces the risk of accidents and collisions. Now rent more jet skis without stress for crashes.
It works in a brilliant way. When the system detects at a distance of approximately 60 meters to another watercraft, IDC lose the throttle and no speed in that range. So you can enjoy riding the way you should.
